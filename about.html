OUR STORY

Built for Salons, By People Who Get It

We started AuraSync because we saw talented salon professionals spending more time wrestling with spreadsheets and phone calls than doing what they love. We built the software we wished existed.

OUR MISSION

Empowering every salon owner to thrive

Our mission is simple: give salon owners the tools, insights, and confidence to run a world-class business. We believe software should work for you — not the other way around.

From a solo nail artist in Auckland to a 15-location hair group in London, every AuraSync client deserves enterprise-grade tools at an accessible price. We're obsessed with making that a reality.
